My Story

Who am I and what I do?

Hello everyone!

My name is David Ristevski, some might know me as “DzaDze”. I come from Macedonia, small but very beautiful country. I currently live in Skopje, the capital of Macedonia.

I’m a Web and Graphic Designer and Illustrator, who does stunning logos, make pixel-perfect and detailed Web design for web and mobile and also Illustrating some lovely and some bad ass characters. In everything I do, I give a piece of my heart and try my best to make the client happy with the final result, and of course make myself happy.

Currently I'm working in the creative direction of NewMedia Marketing, Macedonia.

Also, I am graffiti lover, adventurer and I love love haribo. Dreadlocks on my head forever!
